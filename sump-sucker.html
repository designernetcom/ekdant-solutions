Sump Sucker

A sump sucker is a specialized tool for cleaning the coolant sumps of machine tools. The sump contents usually consist Of coolant & solids. The solids consist of metal chips & swarf, in just a few minutes, a sump sucker will pick up everything in the sump- fluid, as well as solids. The filter separates the solids from the fluid. Filtered coolant is returned to the machine tool sump or transported for additional processing.

Sump Cleaner removes very fine dirt from Coolant upto 1 Op by Vacuum created in the tank. There are no consumables hence no recurring cost. Collected dirt can be easily cleaned & the unit put back to use again

Working Principle

Coolant / Sump Cleaner removes sludge, metal chips & swarf from Coolant tanks & filters it via reusable stainless steel mesh filter. The System works on the Principle of Vacuum Suction. A poweful two stage vacuum blower creates vacuum in the tank of the unit, which sucks in coolant, metal chips. Swarf etc, passing it through mesh basket. Contamination gets in the basket & clean coolant flows through to the tank of the unit. A centrifugal delivery pump delivers back the clean coolant.
