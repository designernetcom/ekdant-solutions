Oil Skimmer

An oil skimmer is a mechanical device that separates oil or particles floating on a liquid surface and are most commonly used for oil spill remediation, as a part of oily water treatment systems, removing oil from machine tool coolant and removing oil from aqueous parts washers. Floating oil is to be extracted for better life of coolant
Application :
  • Used on all CNC machines
  • Conventional machines
  • Washing machines
  • Electro plating grinding machines
